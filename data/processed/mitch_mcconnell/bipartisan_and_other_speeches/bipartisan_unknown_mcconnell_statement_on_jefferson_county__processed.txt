press releases mcconnell statement on jefferson county sheriff john aubrey september 24 , 2025 louisville, ky – u.s. senator mitch mcconnell (r-ky) released the following statement regarding the passing of jefferson county sheriff john aubrey : “i am deeply saddened to hear of the passing of my longtime friend and jefferson county sheriff john aubrey. john was a dedicated public servant who made an indelible impact on our community and for 27 years as sheriff he worked tirelessly to keep our neighborhoods safe. his recognition as the 2025 sheriff of the year was a testament to his unwavering commitment to law enforcement and the respect he earned from all who knew him. elaine and i extend our heartfelt condolences to his family, friends, and the entire jefferson county sheriff's department during this time.” february 4, 2025: senator mcconnell welcomed sheriff aubrey to his office in washington, d.c. permalink: press releases audio statements video statements newsletters photo galleries
