[congressional record volume 170, number 2 (friday, january 5, 2024)] [extensions of remarks] [page e4] from the congressional record online through the government publishing office [ www.gpo.gov ] honoring henry mcginness ______ hon. chuck edwards of north carolina in the house of representatives friday, january 5, 2024 mr. edwards. mr. speaker, i rise today to honor mr. henry mcginness, for his work as an intern serving in my d.c. office. i would like to take this opportunity to thank him for his hard work and recognize the meaningful contributions he has made to the office this fall. henry is from norwalk, connecticut, and is currently a junior at georgetown university where he is studying government. henry is an avid new york sports fan and grew up attending different sporting events in the city. interns work with staff to serve constituents in north carolina's 11th congressional district. henry was chosen from a competitive pool of applicants and exceeded every expectation with his positive attitude and impressive work ethic. it was a pleasure to work with henry, and i am so proud to have such an intelligent and ambitious intern representing my office. henry exemplified the spirit of public service and represents the best that our nation has to offer. i congratulate henry on this successful completion of his internship, and on behalf of the people of western carolina, i thank him.
