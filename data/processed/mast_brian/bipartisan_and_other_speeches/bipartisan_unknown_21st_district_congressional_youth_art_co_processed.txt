press releases print home news press releases nov 07 2025 21st district congressional youth art contest winners announced stuart, fla. – u.s. congressman brian mast (fl-21) proudly recognized the participants of the 2025 21st district congressional youth art competition and announced the winners: arya ortega from beacon cove intermediate, jaylyn eggleton from jensen beach elementary, briley gordon from palm pointe research school, sophia felis-hawkins from trinity christian school of palm beach gardens, trevi gallagher from the pine school, and sarasofia krempler from calvary christian academy.. “each year, the incredible talent of the students in florida’s 21st district amazes me,” said rep. mast . “thank you to all who participated and were willing to share their talents with our community, and congratulations to our winners! i look forward to seeing your work displayed in my office in washington d.c.” the annual 21st district youth art contest showcases the talented elementary and middle school students of the 21st district. there were 230 entries submitted for this year's 21st congressional art contest. this year’s theme is agriculture. share this: related links press releases photos videos
