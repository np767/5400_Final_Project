advisory: sanders and ‘godfather of ai’ to discuss the future of artificial intelligence and impact on humanity november 14, 2025 washington, nov. 14 — sen. bernie sanders (i-vt.) and the “godfather of ai”, nobel laureate dr. geoffrey hinton, will discuss the transformative effects that artificial intelligence (ai) and robotics will have on our country, the world economy and the working class during an event on tuesday, november 18, at 6 p.m. at georgetown university. in october, sanders released a sweeping report warning that artificial intelligence (ai) and automation could eliminate nearly 100 million american jobs over the next decade — while billionaires and corporate executives reap record profits. sanders also published an op-ed in fox news about how to ensure these new technologies benefit every american, not just a handful of billionaires. details what: discussion between sen. sanders and dr. hinton on future of ai when: tuesday, november 18, 6:00 p.m. et where: georgetown university. exact location available upon rsvp. the event will also be streamed on sanders’ social media. who: sen. bernie sanders (i-vt.) dr. geoffrey hinton, professor emeritus, university of toronto; 2024 nobel laureate in physics mo elleithee, executive director, georgetown institute of politics and public service helena monsivais, student, georgetown university rsvp: media wishing to attend in-person must rsvp by emailing please note that the q&a portion of the event is limited to members of the georgetown community.
