Jim Banks Named America’s Toughest Senator on Immigration
WASHINGTON, D.C,
– Today, Immigration Accountability Project Action awarded Senator Jim Banks (R-Ind.) as the #1 U.S. Senator for immigration.
IAP Action calculated the rankings based on the immigration-related bills introduced, cosponsored, and supported by members of Congress. Eligible legislation includes measures addressing legal immigration, nonimmigrant visa programs, border security, interior enforcement, and efforts to combat illegal immigration.
Senator Banks earned the top ranking among all U.S. Senators for the 119th Congress.
Senator Jim Banks: (R-Ind.):
“Hoosiers sent me to the Senate to help President Trump secure the border and put America first. I’m honored to get this recognition and I’ll keep fighting every day to keep our country safe and strong.”
Senator Banks’ Key Immigration Legislative Items here:
Visa Overstay Penalties Act of 2025
Border Security is National Security Act
Putting American Students First Act
End Unaccountable Amnesty Act
Nuclear Family Priority Act
American Tech Workforce Act of 2025
