Jul 19, 2023
Washington, D.C.
— Today, Senator Ted Budd (R-NC) met with Ambassador Kristjan Prikk of Estonia.
Senator Budd said in a statement:
“I’d like to thank Ambassador Prikk for visiting with me today. I thanked him for his nation’s leadership on cybersecurity and defense spending within NATO.  We discussed a number of important issues including fostering innovation, improving security cooperation, and Russia’s brutal war against Ukraine.”
