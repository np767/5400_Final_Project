Order of Procedure
Ms. SMITH. Mr. President, I ask unanimous consent that the cloture
motion with respect to the Szabat nomination be withdrawn and that
following disposition of the Coscia nomination, the Senate immediately
vote on confirmation of the Szabat nomination, with all provisions of
the previous order remaining in effect.
The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
Ms. SMITH. Mr. President, for the information of the Senate, Senators
should expect two rollcall votes starting at 2:15 p.m. We do not expect
additional votes this evening.
