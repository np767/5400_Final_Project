Order of Business
Mr. DURBIN. Madam President, I ask unanimous consent the cloture
motion with respect to the Lund nomination be withdrawn, and
notwithstanding rule XXII, the Senate vote on confirmation of the
nomination at 11:30 a.m. on Thursday, January 25, the year 2024.

Without objection, it is so ordered.
Mr. DURBIN. Madam President, I ask unanimous consent that we move
immediately to the vote that has been scheduled for 2:15.
The PRESIDING OFFICER. Without objection, it is so ordered.
