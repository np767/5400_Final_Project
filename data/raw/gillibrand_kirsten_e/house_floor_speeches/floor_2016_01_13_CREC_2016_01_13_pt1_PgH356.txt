ANNOUNCEMENT BY COMMITTEE ON RULES REGARDING AMENDMENTS TO H.R. 3442,
DEBT MANAGEMENT AND FISCAL RESPONSIBILITY ACT OF 2015
(Mr. SESSIONS asked and was given permission to address the House for
1 minute.)
Mr. SESSIONS. Mr. Speaker, today the Rules Committee issued an
announcement outlining the amendment process for H.R. 3442, the Debt
Management and Fiscal Responsibility Act of 2015. An amendment deadline
has been set for Thursday, January 21, 2016, at 12:00 p.m. That is also
known as noon.
Amendments should be drafted to the text of the bill as reported by
the Committee on Ways and Means, which is posted on the Rules Committee
Web site. Please feel free to contact me or a member of the Rules
Committee if we may be of assistance to any Member in their
preparations.
