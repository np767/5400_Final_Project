PERSONAL EXPLANATION
______
HON. RON KIND
of wisconsin
in the house of representatives
Monday, January 25, 2016
Mr. KIND. Mr. Speaker, I was unable to have my votes recorded on the
House floor on Monday, January 11, 2016 and Tuesday, January 12, 2016.
Had I been present, I would have voted aye on H.R. 598 (Roll no. 34),
H.R. 3231 (Roll no. 35), Kildee Amendment Number 2 to H.R. 1644 (Roll
no. 38), Cartwright Amendment Number 3 (Roll no. 39), Sewell Amendment
Number 4 (Roll no. 40), motion to recommit with instructions (Roll no.
41), and H.R. 757 (Roll no. 43).
I would have voted no on ordering the previous question (Roll no.
36), H. Res. 583 (Roll no. 37), and H.R. 1644 (Roll no. 42).
