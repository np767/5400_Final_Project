TRIBUTE TO DWAYNE BIGGHAM, JR.
Mr. RUBIO. Mr. President, today I recognize Dwayne Biggham,
Jr., a 2018 summer intern in my Orlando office, for all of the hard
work he has done for me, my staff, and the people of the State of
Florida.
Dwayne is a student at Jones High School. He is a dedicated and
diligent worker who has been devoted to getting the most out of his
internship experience.
I would like to extend my sincere thanks and appreciation to Dwayne
Biggham, Jr., for all the fine work he has done and wish him continued
success in the years to come.
