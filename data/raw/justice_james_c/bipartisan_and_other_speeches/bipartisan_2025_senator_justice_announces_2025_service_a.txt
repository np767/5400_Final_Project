Senator Justice Announces 2025 Service Academy Nominees
WASHINGTON, D.C.
– Today, Senator Jim Justice released a statement following his announcement of West Virginia students nominated to attend the United States Service Academies:
“I couldn’t be prouder of these outstanding young West Virginians. Their dedication, leadership, and commitment to service truly embody the best of our state. I have the upmost confidence that they will make West Virginia proud, and they will continue to have my full support. Good luck to our fantastic 2025 service academy nominees,”
said
Senator Jim Justice
.
