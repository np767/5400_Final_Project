MEASURE PLACED ON THE CALENDAR--H.R. 6914
Mr. SCHUMER. Mr. President, I understand that there is a bill at the
desk that is due for a second reading.
The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. The clerk will read the bill by
title for the second time.
The senior assistant legislative clerk read as follows:
A bill (H.R. 6914) to require institutions of higher
education to disseminate information on the rights of, and
accommodations and resources for, pregnant students, and for
other purposes.
Mr. SCHUMER. In order to place the bill on the calendar under the
provisions of rule XIV, I would object to further proceedings.
The ACTING PRESIDENT pro tempore. Objection having been heard, the
bill will be placed on the calendar.
