DOT Awards Over $9 Million to North Dakota Department of Transportation For Roadway Repairs
WASHINGTON, D.C. – The U.S. Department of Transportation (DOT) awarded
$9,413,942
to the North Dakota Department of Transportation through the Federal Highway Administration (FHWA) Emergency Relief (ER) Program. The FHWA’s ER Program provides grants to repair eligible roads, bridges, and infrastructure after natural disasters such as flooding.
